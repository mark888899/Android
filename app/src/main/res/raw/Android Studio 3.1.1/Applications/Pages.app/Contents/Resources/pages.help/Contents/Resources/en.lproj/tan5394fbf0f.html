Send a copy of a document

You can send a copy of a document that recipients can open and modify however they want on their own computers and devices. Their changes don’t affect the original document or any other copies of the document. You can send the copy as a Pages document or in another format—PDF, Microsoft Word, or ePub.

If the original file has a password, it applies to the copy as well, and recipients must know the password to open the copy. You can keep the original password or assign a different password to the copy.

Send a copy in another format

  1. Click a format, then specify settings:

    • Pages: Select the checkbox and add a password if you want to restrict access to the copy.

    • PDF: Click the Image Quality pop-up menu, then choose an image quality (the higher the image quality, the larger the file size of the copy).

    • Word: Click the disclosure triangle next to Advanced Options and choose a format.

    • ePub: Enter the title and author name. If you want your book to appear in a particular category, click the Primary Category pop-up menu, then choose a category. To set the language and book cover and set whether fonts are embedded, click the disclosure triangle next to Advanced Options. You can’t set a password for an ePub file.

  2. Click Next, then provide the requested information (an email address if you’re sending an email, for example).

    If you aren’t signed in to your account for the method you chose, you’re asked to sign in before continuing. For AirDrop, the document is sent when the recipient accepts it.
